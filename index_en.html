Your AI Senere Assistant Beta Testing

Anesthesia AI Senior Consult v0.1-beta

Senere means "later" in Danish. For those moments when you think "Wait, I'll ask about that later" — AI has you covered right away. An anesthesia AI consult app designed for residents and fellows.

💬 Chat Consultation

Ask any question about anesthesia management and the AI senior consultant responds in a structured format with "key points" and "details." Emergency keywords are automatically detected and trigger emergency mode.

⚡ Quick Consult

8 preset emergency scenarios. Access protocols instantly with a single tap.

  • 🚨 Anaphylaxis
  • 🔥 Malignant Hyperthermia
  • 😮 Difficult Airway
  • 💉 Local Anesthetic Systemic Toxicity (LAST)
  • 📉 Intraoperative Hypotension
  • 💓 Bradycardia
  • 🤢 PONV (Postoperative Nausea and Vomiting)
  • 💊 Postoperative Pain Management

💊 Drug Dose Calculator

Automatic weight-based dose calculation for 16 commonly used anesthesia drugs. Enter patient information and see dosages at a glance.

🛡️ Safety Considerations

A disclaimer and consent screen is displayed on first use. In emergency mode, a persistent reminder to "also contact your attending/supervising physician" is always shown. AI is positioned strictly as a supplementary tool.

TOFun Live

Humorous Anesthesiologist v3.0

TOFun is named after TOF = Train-of-Four = 102 — a joke app by anesthesiologists, for anesthesiologists. No registration required. Free to use right in your browser.

🎭 102 Curated Jokes

From OR daily life and surgery room clichés to drug humor — 102 jokes that will make any anesthesiologist smirk. Press the button and a character delivers the joke in a speech bubble.

👍 Voting System

Rate every joke on a 3-point scale.

  • 👍 Good — Like it (+2 points)
  • 🤩 Great — Love it! (+3 points)
  • 😐 Meh — Not great (-1 point)

Jokes are automatically ranked by vote score, so the best ones rise to the top.

✏️ User Submissions

Submit your own original jokes.

  • Simple text input (5–200 characters)
  • Immediately added to the joke rotation after submission
  • Rated by other users through voting
  • Duplicate checks and validation included for peace of mind

🏆 Curation System

An automated system to maintain joke quality.

  • 20 official jokes are always guaranteed to appear
  • The remaining 82 slots are competed for by official and user-submitted jokes based on vote scores
  • Low-rated jokes are automatically replaced, keeping only the funniest ones
  • The app evolves and improves through user voting

🫧 Character Expressions

The TOFun character changes through 10 stages. Its expression changes with each joke you read, making you want to keep going for the next one.

PreVisit Beta Preparation

Remote Preoperative Consultation System

PreVisit is a video consultation app for anesthesiologists to conduct preoperative assessments remotely. WebRTC peer-to-peer communication ensures video is never stored on servers.

📹 Secure Video Calls

Direct peer-to-peer communication via WebRTC — video and audio data never pass through servers, maximizing patient privacy.

💬 Chat Function

Text chat alongside video calls. Convey drug names and instructions accurately via text. Template feature for efficient standard explanations.

🔗 Easy Connection

The doctor creates a room and shares the URL with the patient. No app installation or registration required.
